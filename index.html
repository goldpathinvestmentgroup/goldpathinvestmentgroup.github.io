About Us

Gold Path Investment Group, LLC was founded by Kunal Gupta in 2023. After working in the corporate arena for over a decade, Kunal learned that a large percentage of his colleagues faced challenges in managing short-term financial needs, while also planning for retirement. He became passionate in providing guidance and support to help individuals develop and implement sound financial strategies so they can navigate unexpected emergencies and still live comfortably in retirement. Gold Path works with healthcare professionals, business owners, corporate professionals, military, and other high net worth individuals to help them realize their short and long-term financial goals. Gold Path will also partner with endowments and foundations to help them achieve their short and long-term financial objectives. Every client we manage is based on the principles of integrity, respect, and collaboration.

Investment Process

1. Discovery

Introductory meeting is held to understand the client’s main financial goals, current financial situation, background, time horizon, liquidity constraints, and unique circumstances. Financial and risk tolerance questionnaires are then completed by the client to better understand risk and return requirements.

2. Evaluation

Analysis of client goals, risk and return objectives, time horizon, and all other relevant factors based on the introductory meeting and questionnaire completed by client. Any information that needs additional clarification will be communicated directly to client.

3. Planning

Formulation of an Investment Policy Statement best suited to accomplish the client’s goals and aligned with risk tolerance and all other factors. Action steps to be taken will be outlined and discussed with client during a meeting.

4. Execution

Implement strategy and monitor portfolio holdings, asset allocations, economic climate, as well as asset classes and individual securities that deviate from intrinsic value in the market. Investment opportunities aligned with client goals will be reviewed periodically.

5. Report and Review

Track progress toward goals. Revise strategy and asset allocation, if necessary, based on any changes to client situation, objectives, time horizon, economic climate or other relevant factors. Portfolio review and rebalancing will be conducted annually or more often, if necessary.
