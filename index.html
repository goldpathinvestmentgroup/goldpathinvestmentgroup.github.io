About Us

Gold Path Investment Group was founded by Kunal Gupta in 2023. After working in the corporate arena for over a decade, Kunal learned that a large percentage of his colleagues faced challenges in managing short-term financial needs, while also planning for retirement. He became passionate in providing guidance and support to help individuals develop and implement sound financial strategies so they can navigate unexpected emergencies and still live comfortably in retirement. Gold Path works with doctors, lawyers, business owners, and other high net worth individuals to help them realize their short and long-term financial goals. Gold Path will also partner with endowments and foundations to help them achieve their short and long-term financial objectives. Every client we manage is based on the principles of integrity, respect, and collaboration.

Contact us directly and learn how we can reimagine your financial future.

Asset Allocation

Determine the optimal mix of assets in equity, fixed income, annuities, cash, etc. based on each individual’s risk tolerance, return objectives, and market conditions and adjust the mix, as needed.

Active Management

Continuously monitor the prices of assets across many investment products and combine that with our understanding of market conditions to take advantage of mispricing by markets. We use valuation models and financial analysis to identify these mispriced assets.

Risk Management

Seek to maximize return for the level of risk incurred for each client.

Portfolio Rebalancing

When asset allocations deviate from the target allocations we have set, we will buy and sell assets needed to return your portfolio to the optimal mix.

Investment Process

1. Discovery

Introductory meeting is held to better understand the client’s main financial goals, current financial situation, background, time horizon, and unique circumstances. Questionnaire is then completed by client to provide additional information.

2. Evaluation

Analysis of client goals, risk and return objectives, and all other factors based on the introductory meeting and questionnaire completed by client. Discussion of firm’s approach with client.

3. Planning

Formulation of an investment strategy and/or a financial plan best suited to accomplish the client’s short and long-term goals. Action steps to be taken will be outlined.

4. Execution

Implement strategy and monitor portfolio holdings, asset allocations, economic climate, as well as asset classes and individual securities that deviate from intrinsic value.

5. Report and Review

Track progress toward goals. Revise strategy and asset allocation, if necessary, based on any changes to client situation, objectives, or economic climate.
